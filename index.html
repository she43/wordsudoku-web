The rules

How a board gets solved

  1. Learn the hidden word

    Every puzzle is built around one word — its letters are the only pieces on the board.

  2. Fill without repeats

    Each row, column, and block must contain every letter of that word exactly once.

  3. Tap to place

    Select a cell, then pick a letter from the tray below the grid.

  4. Mind your mistakes

    Three slip-ups and the board resets — unless you revive and keep going.

  5. Beat the clock

    Faster, cleaner solves rank higher on that day's board.
